Quick Tips Thread 💡

• Get a Ventra card for the 'L' train - so convenient!

• Best time to visit: May-June or September-October

• Many museums have free days

• Walking is the best way to explore neighborhoods

• Layer up - it's called the Windy City for a reason! 💨
