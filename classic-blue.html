Discover Chicago

The Windy City awaits with world-class attractions, iconic architecture, and unforgettable experiences

Must-Visit Attractions

Millennium Park

Location: 201 E Randolph St

Home to the iconic Cloud Gate sculpture (The Bean), Crown Fountain, and stunning architecture. Free admission year-round.

Free Art & Culture

The Art Institute of Chicago

Location: 111 S Michigan Ave

One of the oldest and largest art museums in the United States. Features works from around the world spanning 5,000 years.

Museum Art & Culture

Navy Pier

Location: 600 E Grand Ave

Chicago's premier entertainment destination with rides, restaurants, gardens, and spectacular lake views. Don't miss the Ferris wheel!

Family Friendly Entertainment

Willis Tower Skydeck

Location: 233 S Wacker Dr

Experience breathtaking views from 103 floors up. Step onto The Ledge - glass boxes extending 4 feet outside the building!

Views Architecture

Lincoln Park Zoo

Location: 2001 N Clark St

One of the oldest zoos in North America. Home to over 200 species of animals. Free admission!

Free Family Friendly

Shedd Aquarium

Location: 1200 S DuSable Lake Shore Dr

Indoor aquarium with over 32,000 animals. Features beluga whales, penguins, dolphins, and marine life from around the world.

Family Friendly Museum

Chicago Architecture River Cruise

Location: Various departure points

Explore Chicago's world-famous architecture from the Chicago River. Learn about iconic buildings and the city's architectural history.

Architecture Tours

Wrigley Field

Location: 1060 W Addison St

Historic home of the Chicago Cubs since 1914. Catch a game or take a stadium tour of this iconic ballpark.

Sports Historic

Chicago Food Must-Tries

Deep Dish Pizza

Chicago's signature dish with thick crust, chunky tomato sauce, and lots of cheese. Try Lou Malnati's, Giordano's, or Pequod's.

Chicago Hot Dog

All-beef hot dog with mustard, onions, relish, tomato, pickle, peppers, and celery salt. No ketchup! Visit Portillo's or Gene & Jude's.

Italian Beef Sandwich

Thinly sliced roast beef on Italian bread, soaked in gravy. Get it "wet" or "dipped" at Al's Beef or Portillo's.

Garrett Popcorn

Famous Chicago Mix combining caramel and cheese popcorn. Multiple locations throughout the city.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

Late spring (May-June) and early fall (September-October) offer pleasant weather and fewer crowds. Summer is peak tourist season with festivals and events.

Getting Around

The 'L' train system is efficient and affordable. Get a Ventra card for easy access to trains and buses. Uber and Lyft are widely available.

Neighborhoods to Explore

Visit The Loop for downtown attractions, Magnificent Mile for shopping, Wicker Park for hip cafes, and Chinatown for diverse cuisine.

Money-Saving Tips

Many museums offer free admission days. The CityPASS can save money if visiting multiple attractions. Enjoy free concerts at Millennium Park in summer.
