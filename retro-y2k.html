✨ Yummy Chicago Food! ✨

🍕 Deep Dish Pizza

Chicago's MOST FAMOUS food! Thick, cheesy, and totally DELICIOUS! Try Lou Malnati's, Giordano's, or Pequod's!

🌭 Chicago Hot Dog

All-beef hot dog with SEVEN toppings! Remember: NO KETCHUP ALLOWED! Get one at Portillo's or Gene & Jude's!

🥪 Italian Beef

Juicy beef sandwich dipped in gravy - SO GOOD! Order it "wet" or "dipped" at Al's Beef or Portillo's!

🍿 Garrett Popcorn

The famous Chicago Mix - caramel AND cheese popcorn together! You can't eat just one handful!

💡 Super Helpful Tips! 💡

🌞 Best Time to Visit

Come in late spring (May-June) or early fall (September-October) for perfect weather! Summer is festival season but can get crowded!

🚇 Getting Around

Ride the 'L' train - it's fast, cheap, and FUN! Get a Ventra card and explore the whole city! Uber and Lyft work great too!

🗺️ Cool Neighborhoods

Check out The Loop (downtown), Magnificent Mile (shopping!), Wicker Park (hip cafes), and Chinatown (yummy food)!

💰 Save Money!

Many museums have FREE days! Buy a CityPASS to save BIG on attractions! Millennium Park concerts are always FREE!
